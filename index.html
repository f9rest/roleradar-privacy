RoleRadar — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What RoleRadar Does

RoleRadar is a career-matching app that analyses your CV against job descriptions to provide fit scores, interview preparation advice, and professional profile insights.

What Data We Collect

Your CV: When you upload a CV (PDF or text), it is stored locally on your device so you don't need to re-upload it each time. Your CV is never uploaded to our servers or stored externally.

Job descriptions: When you paste or share a job listing URL, the app fetches the webpage content to extract the job description. This content is processed temporarily and not stored externally.

Analysis results: Your fit check results, profile data, and application history are stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party AI Processing

To perform CV analysis, profile generation, and CV improvement, RoleRadar sends your CV content and job description text to Anthropic's Claude API for processing. This data is transmitted securely via HTTPS.

Anthropic's data usage policy applies to this processing. Anthropic does not use API inputs to train their models. You can review Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

What We Don't Do

We do not create user accounts or require registration. We do not collect your name, email address, or personal contact information. We do not store your data on external servers. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party other than Anthropic for AI processing as described above. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

Data Storage

All app data (your CV, profile, analysis history, and improved CV) is stored locally on your device using the app's internal storage. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

Data Deletion

You can delete your stored CV at any time by tapping "Change" on the CV banner. You can delete individual analysis entries by long-pressing them in My Applications. Uninstalling the app deletes all data.

Children's Privacy

RoleRadar is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

Contact
